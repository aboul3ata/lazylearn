# Research Playbook

## Seed first

Start from the user's material. The generated skill should feel like a durable version of the user's own playbook, not a generic internet summary.

## Expand deliberately

Use outside research to fill gaps, validate claims, and bring in primary sources. Do not pad the topic with weak secondary commentary.

## Distill for reuse

The generated skill should preserve:

- recurring decisions
- checklists
- failure modes
- trusted source categories
- useful starter prompts

## Keep the skill operational

Future runs of `/lazylearn-<topic>` should be able to act, not just explain. Prefer:

- decision rules
- audit sequences
- templates
- evaluation criteria

Over:

- motivational language
- broad history
- vague "best practices"
